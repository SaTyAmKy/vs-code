Music

MyTunes is a music-focused platform that brings together users and artists worldwide. It allows listeners to explore a global music library, create and share playlists, discover new artists and cultures, and enjoy a personalized experience of music on the web. MyTunes suggests songs, albums, and artists based on a user’s listening habits and preferences, making it easier to discover new favorites. Each artist has a profile page with their biography, full discography, popular songs, social links, and tour updates, so fans can connect more deeply. Users can quickly find music by searching song names, albums, artists, or genres and can filter results by mood, language, or popularity. Listeners can save favorite songs, albums, and playlists to their account and see their listening history anytime. Users can leave reviews, comment on songs or albums, and interact with others in a global music-loving community.

Video playlists let you stream hours of non-stop entertainment, perfect for parties or relaxation. MyTunes combines sound and visuals to deliver a truly immersive musical entertainment experience.
